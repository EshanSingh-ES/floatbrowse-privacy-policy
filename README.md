# Privacy Policy – FloatBrowse

Effective Date: November 2025

FloatBrowse is a lightweight floating web browser for macOS that allows you to browse the web seamlessly while working in other apps. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

Information We Collect

FloatBrowse does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All browsing activity takes place entirely on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

Third-Party Services

FloatBrowse does not include analytics, advertising, or tracking frameworks. However, websites you visit may collect their own data according to their respective privacy policies.

Data Security

Since FloatBrowse does not collect or store user data, there is no personal information at risk of being exposed or shared.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
