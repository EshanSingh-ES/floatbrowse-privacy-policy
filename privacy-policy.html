Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 2025

MiniBrowse is a lightweight floating web browser for macOS that lets you browse the web without switching windows. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

Information We Collect

MiniBrowse does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All browsing activity occurs locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks. Websites you visit may collect their own data according to their respective privacy policies.

Data Security

Because MiniBrowse does not store or transmit any data, there is no personal information at risk of exposure.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at support@minibrowse.app.
